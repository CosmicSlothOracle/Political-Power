How to Play

Political Card Game is a strategic card game where players compete for influence and power in the political arena.

  • Play cards to gain influence and mandates
  • Form coalitions with other players for mutual benefits
  • Navigate through various game phases including play, effect, resolution, and backfire
  • The first player to reach 61 mandates wins the game
